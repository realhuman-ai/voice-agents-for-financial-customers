LLMs are capable of a variety of tasks, such as:
1. Text generation: Creating human-like text based on a prompt.
2. Language translation: Translating text between languages.
3. Sentiment analysis: Determining the sentiment expressed in a piece of text.
4. Summarization: Condensing large volumes of information into shorter summaries.
5. Question answering: Providing direct answers to user queries.
6. Code generation: Writing programming code in various languages.
7. Conversational AI: Powering chatbots and virtual assistants for natural interactions.
8. Creative writing: Assisting in story writing, poetry, or script creation.
