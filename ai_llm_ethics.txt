Ethical concerns surrounding LLMs include:
1. Bias and fairness: The risk of perpetuating societal biases embedded in training data.
2. Privacy: The possibility of models inadvertently revealing sensitive information present in their training datasets.
3. Misinformation: The potential to generate and spread false or misleading information.
4. Job displacement: Automation of tasks traditionally performed by humans, leading to economic disruption.
5. Misuse: The risk of malicious applications, such as generating deepfake content or deceptive phishing messages.
6. Accountability: Challenges in assigning responsibility for decisions or actions taken by AI systems.
